Nigel Mansell on the future of F1

As the Formula One season draws to a close, Nigel Mansell looks to the future and which teams may be able to compete with Mercedes next season. Ferrari, McLaren and Williams must all make huge improvements in a bid to challenge Lewis Hamilton and Nico Rosberg in 2016. Mansell also talks about the rumours surrounding the future of Red Bull and Toro Rosso

Ryan Baxter and Dan Castella, Source: guardian.co.uk
