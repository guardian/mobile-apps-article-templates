About this content
The podders show some love to European football's much-maligned secondary competition. Plus, full previews of the weekend's action in the Premier League
Advertisement

Never mind the Champions League - on today's earlier-than-usual Football Weekly Extra , Dave Farrar, Jacob Steinberg , James Horncastle and Paul MacInnes get hot and sweaty about the Uefa's second-favourite son, the Europa League.

Yes, we look forward to such epic clashes as Liverpool v Besiktas , Celtic v Inter , Tottenham v Fiorentina and, er, Wolfsburg v Sporting. So take that, Chelsea and your 1-1 draw at PSG!

Plus, we ask whether there is actually some sense in Brentford's decision to ask Mark Warburton to do one at the end of the season , and we give you a typically thorough lookahead to all the big games this weekend in the Premier League, including Hull v QPR and Tim Sherwood's first game in charge at Aston Villa as they host Stoke.

Jimbo will be back on Monday, and if you still haven't caught up with Barry's in-depth interviews with Le Tiss, the Goat, Mr Em and James 'Beats' Beattie, then the only place you can hear them is Acast .
