Max Rushden steps in for AC Jimbo, as the pod discuss a thriller at the Emirates, Hull stunning Leicester, and whether Pep put full-backs in midfield

With AC Jimbo in Botswana to watch some more of The World’s Strongest Men, Max Rushden sits in to review the first weekend of action in this new Premier League season, with Iain Macintosh, Paul Macinnes and Simon Burnton.

With the supply teacher in, it’s not long before analysis gives way to more hypothetical chat, such as why there isn’t a five-a-side competition in the Olympic Games and why ET is more Kew Gardens than Piccadilly Circus.
